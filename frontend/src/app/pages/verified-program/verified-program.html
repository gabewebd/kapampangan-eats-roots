Verified Program

Spotting the "Verified Authentic" badge means you're looking at a true cornerstone of Angeles City's cultural and culinary identity.

What is the "Verified Authentic" Badge?

The badge is awarded to eateries and heritage sites that pass our Authenticity Scoring Framework (ASF). This isn't just a rating; it's a measure of cultural impact and historical continuity.

The ASF Criteria

Historical Continuity

Years in operation and persistence of core traditions across generations.

Cultural Authenticity

Adherence to traditional recipes, ancestral methods, and genuine local materials.

Community Relevance

The role of the establishment as a gathering place and point of pride for locals.

Heritage Documentation

Presence of artifacts, archival photos, or well-documented oral histories.

How to Get Verified

  1. Submit a Listing: Ensure all cultural stories and historical data are complete.
  2. Admin Review: Our team vets the submission for factual accuracy and image quality.
  3. ASF Assessment: The establishment is scored against the four pillars of authenticity.
  4. Badge Awarded: Once a high threshold is met, the "Verified Authentic" badge appears on the listing.
