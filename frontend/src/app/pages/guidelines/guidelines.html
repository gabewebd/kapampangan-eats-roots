Community Guidelines

Our mission is to map the true flavors and cultural heritage of Angeles City. These guidelines help ensure that our community remains a trusted source for authentic experiences.

1. Respect Cultural Heritage

When submitting a "Heritage Site" or describing a "Local Eatery," focus on the historical and cultural significance. Respect the stories told by elders and help preserve the narrative quality of our community.

2. Accuracy is Key

  • Locations: Ensure the map pin is placed accurately.
  • Details: Provide correct years in operation and historical context.
  • Authenticity Traits: Only list traits that are genuinely present (e.g., traditional cooking methods, ancestral recipes).

3. High-Quality Media

Upload clear, original photos that show the essence of the spot. Avoid blurry images, screenshots from other websites, or promotional flyers. We want to see the real Angeles!

4. Honest Participation

Avoid self-promotion solely for commercial gain. Submissions should be made with the intent of sharing cultural value. Fake reviews or misleading information will be removed immediately.
