Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 24, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Angeles Eats & Roots. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register on the website (Name, Email, Password).
  • Submit a vendor listing (Business details, images, cultural stories).
  • Contact us through our forms.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Maintain and manage your user account.
  • Verify and publish your vendor submissions.
  • Improve the user experience and functionality of the "Explore Map".
  • Communicate with you regarding your submissions or account status.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using MongoDB Atlas. Passwords are encrypted using modern hashing algorithms. While we strive to protect your personal information, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to enhance our application:

  • Cloudinary: For hosting and processing images you upload.
  • Leaflet/OpenStreetMap: For providing the interactive map experience.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal information at any time. To do so, please log in to your profile or contact our administrator.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this policy, you may contact the Angeles Eats & Roots team through our website or directly at the university project coordinator.
